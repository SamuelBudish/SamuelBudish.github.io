Home-made Oreo Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 (18 ounce) boxes devil's food cake mix
  • 4 eggs, beaten well
  • 0.6 cup oil
  • 0.5 cup margarine
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 cups icing sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. Mix cake mix, eggs and oil together well.
  2. Shape into marble size balls.
  3. Bake at 350F for 10 minutes. They are done when the tops looked cracked.
  4. Mix margarine, cream cheese, icing sugar and vanilla together.
  5. When cookies are completely cooled, frost the flat side of one cookie and then sandwich with another cookie.
